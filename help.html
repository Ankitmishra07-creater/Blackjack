ABOUT

This is a simple classic cassino game.
Earlier it was most famous game in Europian countries.

INSTRUCTIONS

  • The main target is to make the sum of cards 21 to get a blackjack.
  • To start game at first click on "PLAY" in navigation bar or click on "PLAY GAME" on screen.
  • After that the game page will open.Afterwards,click on "START GAME",two cards will be drawn automattically.
  • If the sum is "21",you will WON.
  • In case if you get less than 21,you will get one more chance to draw a new card untill you get sum greater or equal to "21". By clicking on "NEW CARD" you can draw a new card.
